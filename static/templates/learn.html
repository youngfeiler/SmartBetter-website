Explain this to me as if I was 3 years old

Let's explain the concept by looking at an example of a Positive EV game. Let's say we flip a coin; if the coin shows up heads, then I give you $1.50. If the coin shows up tails, then you give me $1.00. The coin is 50/50 to show up heads vs tails, so this would be an easy decision for you. Half the time, you'll make $1.50 in profit. The other half, you'll lose $1.00. The expected value = 50% x $1.50 - 50% x $1.00 = $0.25. Sure, the coin will land on tails sometimes & you'll lose $1.00, but long-term you'll make a lot of money playing this coin flip game. This is a Positive EV game for you, and you should take this bet as many time as possible.
