Sports betting 101

A good place to start. Get the low-down before you dive in.

What is a line?

Lines are the odds set by sportsbooks for betting on a sporting event. For example, if the moneyline is Broncos -150 vs Patriots +130, you would have to bet $150 on the Broncos to win $100 or bet $100 on the Patriots to win $130.

What is a bankroll?

A bankroll is the total amount of money a bettor sets aside strictly for sports betting, which allows them to make wagers based on their personal finances and risk tolerance.

What is a unit?

A unit is a standardized betting amount used by bettors to manage a bankroll. They can scale bet sizes up or down -- in units -- based on their confidence, having each unit represent the same % of bankroll.

What is EV?

Expected value (EV) in sports betting is a mathematical concept that represents the potential profit or loss a bettor can expect to make over the long term. It's calculated by multiplying the probability of winning a bet by the potential profit and subtracting the probability of losing multiplied by the potential Â loss.

What does +EV mean?

+EV, short for Positive Expected Value, in sports betting indicates that a bet is statistically favorable; it refers to a concept in decision theory and probability that denotes a situation or choice where the expected outcome, in terms of profit, is positive. In other words, when a bet has a +EV, it indicates that, on average, it is expected to yield a net gain or advantage over time across a large number of observations.

How does +EV betting profit in the long run?

By consistently seeking out bets with a positive expected value, bettors increase their chances of profiting in the long term. The key to +EV betting is the statistical advantage it provides over a series of bets. Over time, as more bets are placed, the cumulative effect of this advantage tends to result in a profit. However, it's crucial to manage one's bankroll and exercise discipline to withstand short-term losses and capitalize on the long-term benefits of +EV betting.
