  • Team to bet on
    The team in bold is the team to place your Moneyline bet on
  • Sportsbook With Best Odds
    The sportsbook which had the best odds posted at the time the AI reommended placing this wager. Other sportsbooks may have similiar odds or better at this point in time.
  • Recommended Bet Size
    Recommended size of your wager based on the Kelley Criterion and the size of your bankroll. This is a common sizing technique used by professional gamblers. Please change your wagering bankroll the settings page.
  • Minimum Accepted Odds
    The lowest odds our model finds value placing this wager at. Aim to place your wagers at or above this line.
  • Best Odds
    The highest odds at the time our model found value in placing this wager. Aim to place your wagers near this line.
  • % +EV
    The % of positive expected value this bet contains relative to market implied probabilities. This value is calculated from the "best odds" line.
  • Game Date
  • Time Since Update
    Time elapsed since our AI found this bet with the posted odds
  • Add to Bet Tracker
    Clicking this button allows you to input details of a bet you placed. This allows the model to optimize your wagering based on previous bets you've placed for this game, as well as allows you to track your results and view your wagering history.
